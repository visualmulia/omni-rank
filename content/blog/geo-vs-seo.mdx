# GEO vs SEO: The Key Differences You Need to Know

For the last two decades, Search Engine Optimization (SEO) has been the undisputed king of digital marketing. However, with the rise of Large Language Models (LLMs) and conversational search, a new player has emerged: **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)**.

If you don't adjust your content strategy now, your brand risks becoming completely invisible in AI answers.

<StatBox value="5.8x" label="higher conversion rate for AI search traffic" />

## Understanding the Shift

Traditional SEO is built on keywords, links, and domain authority. Google's goal is to show a list of blue links that match the user's query.

GEO, on the other hand, is built on **trust, structured data, and clarity**. AI engines don't want to show links; they want to answer the query directly. They compile information from multiple sources and cite the ones that provide the highest evidence density.
