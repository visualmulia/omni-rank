                    OMNIRANK: THE COMPLETE GEO CHECKLIST 2026
           25 Actionable Steps to Optimize Your Brand for AI Search
================================================================================

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of optimizing your website 
and brand presence so Large Language Models (LLMs) like ChatGPT, Claude, and 
Gemini cite and recommend you in search answers.

Follow these 25 steps to maximize your brand's AI search visibility.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 1: TECHNICAL CRAWLABILITY (6 Steps)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] 1. Create a root-level /llms.txt file to guide AI crawler models.
[ ] 2. Establish a /llms-full.txt file for detailed project files and context.
[ ] 3. Explicitly allow AI bots (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot) in robots.txt.
[ ] 4. Embed clean, schema-compliant JSON-LD markup on every article.
[ ] 5. Implement structured FAQPage schemas for informational queries.
[ ] 6. Ensure your XML Sitemap contains dateModified attributes for freshness.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 2: CONTENT STRUCTURE & EVIDENCE DENSITY (7 Steps)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] 7. Use the "Answer-First" model: Place a 30-40 word summary at the top.
[ ] 8. Focus on Evidence Density: Incorporate statistics, studies, and data points.
[ ] 9. Format key takeaways into clear bullet points for easy AI extraction.
[ ] 10. Write in Self-Contained Units (SCUs): Short, fact-heavy paragraphs.
[ ] 11. Use explicit HTML IDs (e.g. id="step-1") on headings for anchor citations.
[ ] 12. Structure comparison data using transparent HTML tables.
[ ] 13. Answer questions directly: Create a dedicated FAQ section with H3 tags.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 3: BRAND ENTITY & OFF-PAGE AUTHORITY (6 Steps)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] 14. Standardize your Name, Address, and Phone (NAP) across the entire web.
[ ] 15. Create or claim profiles on third-party directories (G2, Trustpilot).
[ ] 16. Secure references in authoritative publications to feed AI training sets.
[ ] 17. Maintain active GitHub, LinkedIn, and social profiles (social proof).
[ ] 18. Build Wikipedia or Wikidata entity references if eligible.
[ ] 19. Ensure your brand is mentioned alongside your target keyword categories.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 4: MONITORING & ONGOING AUDITING (6 Steps)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] 20. Run weekly GEO Visibility Audits using OmniRank to check your overall grade.
[ ] 21. Track your AI Share of Voice (SOV) across Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Gemini.
[ ] 22. Log and monitor search console keywords to trace AI crawler overlaps.
[ ] 23. Test query prompts manually inside LLM chatbots to check citations.
[ ] 24. Optimize low-performing pages with high-impact prioritized action plans.
[ ] 25. Update outdated content periodically to trigger freshness signals.
